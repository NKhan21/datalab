Passage of a Continuing Resolution has the impact of increasing total contract spending by over 4 billion dollars for a given week all else held equal.

The passage of a budget did not have statistically significant effect on the level of total contract spending, all else held constant. We also looked at how the Congress passing a Budget or Continuing Resolutions might impact specific types of contracts.
