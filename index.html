Federal Income Explorer

Where does the money come from?

In 2017, the Federal Government collected $3.3 trillion. If you lived or worked in the United States during this time, you likely contributed to this total in some capacity. Believe it or not, taxes aren’t the only source of federal income. Discover the various sources of income, examine trends over time, and compare income of the U.S. to other countries.

We wondered what we could learn by analyzing ten years of USAspending data on federal contract spending.
