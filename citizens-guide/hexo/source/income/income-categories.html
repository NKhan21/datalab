The $3.3 trillion the government collected last year keeps the federal government in operation.

Most government income collected comes from individual taxpayers, small businesses, and corporations through taxes that get collected on a yearly or quarterly basis.

Other sources of income include money collected from excise taxes, estate taxes, and customs duties. Government income also comes from payments to federal agencies like the Post Office who sell products or services to the public, like stamps. Have you visited a national park recently?
