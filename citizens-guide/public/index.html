The People's Guide to U.S. Federal Government Finances

This site was designed for the people of the United States. The site provides information and data about the annual financial picture of the United States to the public.

Where does the money come from? Where does it go? And what are the long-term obligations of the federal government's financial decisions? We've collected information from various Treasury data sources, to try and answer these questions for you.
