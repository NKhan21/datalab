The $3.3 trillion the government collected last year keeps the federal government in operation.

The majority of government income collected comes from individual taxpayers and small businesses. The second largest category comes from those taxes that come directly from your paycheck. These taxes support programs such as Social Security, Medicare, and Unemployment compensation.

Other sources of income include revenue collected from excise taxes, estate taxes, and customs duties. Government income also comes from payments made to federal agencies like the Post Office who sell products or services to the public, like stamps.
